Materials We Buy

We specialize in non-ferrous metals for our smelting and refining research. Here's what we actively purchase across Middle TN and Southern KY.

🟠
Copper
COMEX: HG1! — Priced per pound

Copper is our primary purchase metal and the backbone of our smelting research. We buy all grades of copper — from bare bright wire to lower-grade insulated wire, tubing, and pipe. Copper is priced per pound at 50–60% of the current COMEX HG1 spot price, adjusted for grade and cleanliness.

Bare Bright Wire #1 Copper #2 Copper Copper Tubing Copper Pipe Insulated Wire Copper Fittings HVAC Copper
Highest value for clean, uninsulated bare bright copper. Insulated wire and lower grades pay at the appropriate grade-adjusted rate.
⬜
Silver
COMEX: SI1! — Priced per troy oz

Silver is a special purchase for our metallurgy research — we buy both industrial silver and sterling scrap. If you have sterling flatware, industrial contacts, photographic silver, or silver-bearing electronics, we are active buyers. Silver is priced per troy ounce at 50–60% of the COMEX SI1 spot price.

Sterling Silver (925) Fine Silver Industrial Contacts Silver Solder Silver Flatware Photographic Silver
Sterling (925) paid at 92.5% of fine silver weight. Industrial silver content verified on-site before pricing.
🔵
Aluminum
NYMEX: ALI1! — Priced per pound

Aluminum is abundant in the residential and construction materials we encounter — siding, window frames, rims, and extrusions. We buy clean aluminum at 50–60% of the NYMEX ALI1 spot price per pound. Aluminum is lightweight but adds up quickly by volume, making it especially worth picking up on larger loads.

Aluminum Siding Aluminum Rims/Wheels Window Frames Extruded Aluminum Cast Aluminum Aluminum Cans (bulk) HVAC Fins
Clean, uncoated aluminum pays at the highest rate. Painted or coated aluminum is graded accordingly.
🟡
Brass & Mixed Non-Ferrous
Spot-Referenced — Priced per pound

Brass is a copper-zinc alloy that turns up in plumbing, HVAC, and hardware. We buy yellow brass, red brass, and mixed brass at competitive rates. We also purchase other non-ferrous metals including bronze, lead (in qualified quantities), and clean stainless steel on a case-by-case basis.

Yellow Brass Red Brass Plumbing Fittings Brass Valves Shell Casings Bronze Stainless (case-by-case)
Brass rates vary by alloy content. We identify and grade on-site before quoting — no mystery deductions.
