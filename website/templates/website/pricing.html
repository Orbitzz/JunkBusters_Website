Load-Based Pricing

We use a 5'×7'×16' enclosed trailer as our measuring unit. A full trailer = 20 cubic yards — roughly equivalent to 6–8 pickup truck loads.

What's a "1/8 trailer"?
Our trailer holds ~6–8 pickup truck beds of junk. So 1 pickup truck load ≈ 1/8 trailer ($210), half our trailer ≈ 3–4 trucks ($525), and a full trailer holds it all ($875). When we arrive, we'll point to exactly where your load lands before loading begins.

Pricing FAQs

Do I pay before or after the job? +

You pay after the job is complete. We assess the load size before we start, confirm the price with you, then get to work. No payment is collected until you're satisfied.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept cash, check, and all major credit/debit cards. Remember: cash earns a 5% discount.

Are there any hidden fees? +

None. Our price includes labor, landfill fees, taxes, and transport. Special-item surcharges (mattresses, tires, construction debris) are disclosed before we begin. You'll always know your total before a single item is touched.

Can I get a price estimate before you arrive? +

Yes! Use our online quote form or call 615-881-2505. Send us photos and a rough description — we'll give you a ballpark before we even show up.

What areas do you serve? +

We cover Middle Tennessee (Nashville, Clarksville, Hendersonville, Gallatin, Springfield, White House, Franklin, Goodlettsville, Portland) and Southern Kentucky (Bowling Green area). Robertson, Sumner, and Montgomery County customers get an automatic 10% discount.
