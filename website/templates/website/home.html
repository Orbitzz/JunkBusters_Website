About Us

Junk Busters LLC is a family-owned business dedicated to providing premier debris removal and professional cleaning across Middle Tennessee and Southern Kentucky. While our fleet is stationed in Orlinda, we maintain a dedicated office in the heart of Nashville to better serve our growing community.

What started with a single truck has grown into a regional operation built on a simple commitment: delivering reliable, affordable service with a neighborly touch. Whether you need a single-item pickup in White House, a full estate clean-out in Clarksville, or commercial services in Bowling Green, our background-checked and insured crew handles every job with professionalism and care.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our junk removal services

How much does junk removal cost in Nashville?

Junk Busters pricing starts at $75 and is based on the volume of junk removed. We offer free, no-obligation quotes — call 615-881-2505 or request a quote online.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Middle Tennessee and Southern Kentucky, including Nashville, Clarksville, White House, Hendersonville, Gallatin, Springfield, Franklin, Goodlettsville, Portland, Bowling Green KY, and surrounding areas within a 50-mile radius of Orlinda, TN.

What types of junk do you remove?

We remove furniture, appliances, electronics, yard waste, construction debris, estate cleanouts, garage cleanouts, hot tubs, sheds, and scrap metal. We do not remove hazardous materials such as paint, chemicals, or asbestos.

How quickly can you pick up my junk?

We offer same-day and next-day appointments in most cases. Book online or call 615-881-2505 to schedule.

Do you offer estate cleanouts?

Yes. Junk Busters specializes in full estate cleanouts, foreclosure cleanouts, and eviction cleanouts throughout Middle TN and Southern KY. Contact us for a free quote.
